Fed up with the government’s “indifference” to their pleas to provide better civic amenities including filling potholes, ‘Citizens Group, East Bengaluru’ on Sunday launched a ‘NoDevelopmentNoTax’, a campaign to boycott paying property tax.
Last week, members of the group got together and contributed funds to fix potholes on a 6km stretch in Halanayakanahalli, Muneshwara Layout and Choodasandra.
